## SI-scaling read
- Claims/servicing metrics are **insurer-level — identical regardless of the SI tier** you buy; a ₹10L and a ₹1Cr Optima Secure+ are adjudicated by the same in-house team on the same TAT.
- Only *practical* SI effect: larger claims (high-SI) draw more scrutiny/investigation under §1.3.c-d, but the no-sub-limit / no-co-pay structure (M2) keeps deductions low.
