## Benefit-stacking math (Base SI = **B**)
| Layer | This plan | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Base SI | B | room + ICU **At Actuals** |
| Secure Benefit (instant 2x) | +100% of B | usable after base exhausted; brochure "2x from day 1". Unused ≠ carried forward |
| Infinite Benefit (annual) | +100% of B **each completed year, uncapped, carries forward, irrespective of claims** | Yr2 +B, Yr3 +2B … no accrual limit |
| Automatic Restore | +100% of B, **unlimited times/yr**, instant on exhaustion; usable for same or unrelated illness | can't push a *single* claim past the cap below |
| **Per-claim cap rule** | one claim ≤ Base + remaining Secure + remaining CB/Plus + remaining Infinite | Restore refills *between* claims, not within one |
| Attaches to | **only Base Coverage (B-1) + Protect Benefit (B-2.3)** | not to optional covers (e-opinion, global) |

_Effect: a ₹10L Secure+ gives ~₹20L usable in Yr1 (Base+Secure) + ₹10L restore per claim, growing ₹10L every renewal via Infinite — with **no room-rent cap even at the ₹10L tier**._

## SI-scaling read (coverage generality across tiers)
- **Room At Actuals at *every* tier incl. ₹10L** — the usual "small SI ⇒ room cap" penalty simply doesn't exist here; this is the standout.
- **Secure (2x) + Infinite (100%/yr)** scale with base, so even a modest ₹10–25L base compounds into large cover over a 26-yo's horizon with no claim-linked bonus loss.
- **Flat, non-scaling caps:** air ambulance ₹5L and daily cash ₹800/day are the same at ₹10L and ₹2Cr — trivial at high SI, minor at low.
- Preventive check-up is the only benefit that meaningfully steps up with SI.

## Module 1 score: **5 / 5**
Best-in-class coverage architecture: no room-rent cap at any SI tier, inbuilt consumables (Protect Benefit), an **uncapped bonus that ignores claims** (Infinite), and unlimited restore. The per-claim cap and the flat ₹5L air-ambulance / ₹800 daily-cash sub-limits are the only minor coverage ceilings; global cover and maternity are optional/add-on rather than inbuilt. For an individual seeking comprehensive indemnity, this is the benchmark the other four finalists must beat.
