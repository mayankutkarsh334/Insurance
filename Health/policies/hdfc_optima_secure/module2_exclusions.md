# Module 2 — Exclusions & Waiting Periods

## Claim-lever definitions (extract first)
| Definition | This plan | Why it bites |
|------------|-----------|--------------|

## Waiting periods & restrictions
| Item | Detail | Concern |
|------|--------|---------|
| Initial (30-day) waiting | **30 days** (Excl03); accidents exempt; waived if continuous coverage >12 mo | standard |
| PED waiting | **36 months** (Excl01); reduces 1 yr per continuous policy year | reducible to 24/12 mo **only at channel level — NOT available to individual buyers** (§2.12) |
| Specific-disease waiting (list) | **24 months** (Excl02) — cataract, hernia, joint replacement, ENT/tonsils, D&C, hysterectomy, fibroids, piles/fissure/fistula, gallbladder, kidney/bladder stones, benign tumours/cysts/polyps/breast lumps, PCOD, GERD, all cirrhosis, pancreatitis, osteoarthritis/osteoporosis, BPH, ligament/tendon/meniscal tear, endometriosis, prolapse, varicose veins, glaucoma, retinal detachment | not for accidents; **longer of PED/specific applies** |
| Enhancement-of-SI resets waiting | **Yes** — both PED (Excl01.ii) and specific-disease (Excl02.ii) waiting **apply afresh on the increased portion** | easy to miss when topping up SI |
| Co-pay | **None** — no mandatory co-pay (Def 8 exists but not applied in Secure+) | ✅ excellent |
| Sub-limits / disease capping | **None** — room & ICU At Actuals, no disease-wise caps | ✅ only AYUSH carries a schedule sub-limit |
| Modern-treatment sub-limits (12 mandated) | **No sub-limit found in wording → covered up to SI** (robotic surgery etc.) | verify latest Annexure A; insurer & Ditto confirm no cap |
| Mental-illness coverage (parity?) | **Covered up to SI, no sub-limit** (no exclusion; insurer-confirmed incl. HIV) | parity OK |
| Zone-based co-pay | **None** — clause 1.24 "Premium Tier" sets *premium* by city (Tier 1–6), **not** co-pay; treatment in a higher tier triggers no co-pay | brochure "No Geography-Based Co-payment" **confirmed** by wording (feeds M4 pricing) |
| Deductible / aggregate deductible | **Optional only** — Aggregate Deductible ("Value Buy") for premium discount; base has **no** deductible | |
| Permanent exclusions (full list) | Standard IRDAI set (Excl04–18) + specific exclusions: war/nuclear, self-injury/suicide, congenital external defects, stem-cell harvesting, OPD, hearing aids/spectacles/contact lenses, alopecia, artificial limbs/durable equipment (unless accident/intra-op), refractive error <7.5D, sterility/infertility, maternity (add-on) | ~standard — nothing unusually harsh |
| Underwriting-imposed permanent exclusion on declared conditions | **Possible** (§1.21 + Excl C.3.q) — via counter-offer the insurer may permanently exclude a declared condition, add specific waiting, or load up to **100%/condition, 150%/person** | accepted proposal can still carve out your PED — check counter-offer letter |
| Non-disclosure consequences | Void/forfeit for misrepresentation; 3 remedies for PED non-disclosure; 60-mo moratorium | detail in Module 6 |

## SI-scaling read
- Waiting periods and exclusions are **identical across all SI tiers** — 36-mo PED / 24-mo specific / no co-pay / no sub-limits apply whether you buy ₹10L or ₹1Cr.
- The one SI-linked trap is **enhancement-of-SI resets waiting on the increase** — buying a small base now and enlarging later re-triggers PED/specific waiting on the added cover, so for a 26-yo a larger base *up front* avoids resetting the clock.

## Module 2 score: **4 / 5**
Exceptionally clean restriction profile — **zero co-pay, zero disease sub-limits, no zone co-pay, modern treatments & mental illness up to SI, deductible only if you opt in**. Held back from 5 by a **36-month PED wait** (vs 24 months at best-in-class finalists like Care Supreme/SBI) that an **individual buyer cannot shorten** (channel-only), plus the standard enhancement-of-SI waiting reset. Strong, but PED-timing is its one genuine relative weakness.
