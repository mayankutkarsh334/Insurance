# Module 1 — Coverage & Benefits

_Source: **binding policy wording** `Activ One` (UIN ADIHLIP27048V022627, `resources/`) + brochure. The documents supplied are the **NXT variant**. Wording governs._

> **Read this first — plain-English framing**
> "Coverage" = the list of things the policy will pay for, and how big the pot is. This module checks what Activ One's cheapest rung (NXT) actually includes, compared with our benchmark (HDFC Optima Secure+, scored 4.65/5).

> ⚠️ **Important variant caveat.** "Activ One" is **one product with several rungs** — NXT, MAX, VIP+, SAVR. Our shortlist picked **"MAX"** (consumables inbuilt, bonus to 500%), but the documents in `resources/` are **NXT**, the entry rung. Several headline features are **weaker or absent in NXT** (see below). To score the plan we actually shortlisted, the **MAX wording is needed** — findings here are for NXT and flagged where MAX likely differs.

## Plain-English glossary
| Term | In simple words |
|------|-----------------|
| **Sum Insured (SI)** | The yearly pot the insurer will pay from |
| **Super Reload** | Refills the pot back to full if you use it up — so one big year doesn't leave you with nothing |
| **Super Credit** | A yearly bonus that grows your pot (up to 6× in 6 years) even if you don't claim |
| **Claim Protect** | Pays the "non-medical" bits of a bill (gloves, masks, etc.) insurers normally skip |
| **HealthReturns** | Cash-back you *earn* for staying active — usable to pay premiums/bills |
| **Room rent "at actuals"** | No cap on your hospital room cost (no nasty proportionate cut) |

## SI-scaling read
- **Room at actuals at every tier** (incl ₹10L) — like HDFC, the small-SI room-cap trap is avoided by default.
- SI ceiling runs to **₹6Cr** (vs HDFC ₹2Cr) — more headroom for very high cover.
- The weak spots (no inbuilt consumables, optional bonus) are **the same at every SI** — they're variant limits, not size limits.

## Module 1 score: **4 / 5** *(for the NXT variant supplied)*
Broad, modern coverage — **no room cap, no sub-limits, inbuilt modern treatments + mental illness + HIV, 2X day-1, 90-day pre-hosp (beats HDFC), unlimited Super Reload, and the unique HealthReturns earn-back**. Held below the HDFC benchmark because two things HDFC gives **free/inbuilt are paid options in NXT**: **consumables (Claim Protect)** and the **growth bonus (Super Credit, capped at 500%/₹3Cr)**. **The shortlisted MAX variant likely scores higher** (consumables inbuilt) — obtain MAX docs to score the intended plan.
